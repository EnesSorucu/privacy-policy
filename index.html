To provide a functional and improved gaming experience, we integrate the following third-party services. These services may collect and process certain data as described below:

3. Data Retention & Security

Since the app operates primarily offline and stores most data locally, we do not maintain a central database of your personal gaming history. Any data processed by our third-party partners (RevenueCat and AdMob) is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

Because we do not host personal profiles on our servers, you can exercise your right to "be forgotten" simply by deleting the application, which will erase all locally stored data.
